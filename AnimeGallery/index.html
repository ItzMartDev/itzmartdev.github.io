Shikimori's Not Just a Cutie

2022

Shikimori San

Main Protagonist

A beautiful and popular girl who has a cool and protective side. Despite her sweet appearance, she proves to be strong and determined when necessary.

Izumi Yuu

Male Protagonist

A kind and gentle boy, but extremely unlucky. Although Shikimori often saves him, he deeply admires her and wants to become someone who can protect her as well.

Hachimitsu Yui

Friend of the couple

A calm and observant girl who often makes sarcastic comments. Although she doesn't show many emotions, she is very loyal to her friends.

Nekozaki Kyou

Shikimori's friend

Active and sporty, she is one of Shikimori's best friends. She admires Shikimori’s strength and enjoys watching the couple’s relationship.

Inuzuka Shu

Izumi’s best friend

Outgoing and cheerful, he supports Izumi and cares about his happiness.

Komi Can't Communicate

2021 - 2022

Komi Shouko

Main Protagonist

An extremely beautiful and elegant student who has great difficulty communicating with others. Her goal is to make 100 friends, and with Tadano’s help, she starts overcoming her social anxiety.

Tadano Hitohito

Male Protagonist

A normal boy who notices Komi's struggles with communication. He decides to help her achieve her goal of making friends.

Najimi Osana

Everyone’s best friend

An extremely extroverted and sociable person with a mysterious past. They can befriend anyone easily.

Yamai Ren

Obsessive fan of Komi

A student who has an unhealthy obsession with Komi, sometimes acting like a stalker.

Agari Himiko

Shy student

Initially very insecure, she finds comfort in being around Komi.

Katai Makoto

Muscular but shy boy

Despite his intimidating appearance, he is kind and also struggles with socializing.

Alya Sometimes Hides Her Feelings in Russian

2024

Masashika Kuze

Male Protagonist

A laid-back and carefree boy who understands Russian and always catches Alya’s true intentions, though he pretends not to understand to tease her.

Alya (Alisa Mikhailovna Kujou)

Main Protagonist

A half-Russian, half-Japanese student known for her beauty and intelligence. She often acts cold and distant but occasionally lets slip affectionate phrases in Russian when near Masachika, revealing her true feelings.

Maria Mikhailovna Kujou

Alya’s younger sister

Unlike her sister, Maria is outgoing and friendly, loving to tease and play with Masachika.

Yuki Suou

Masachika’s Childhood Friend

A friendly and outgoing girl who has known Masachika since childhood. She has a competitive streak and sometimes clashes with Alya.

Sayaka Taniyama

Masachika’s Friend

A lively and cheerful girl who loves gossip and enjoys watching Alya and Masachika’s interactions.

The Tunnel to Summer, the Exit of Goodbyes

2022

Kaoru Touno

Main Protagonist

A lonely young man who discovers a mysterious tunnel that can grant wishes at the cost of accelerating time. He is haunted by his past and the loss of his sister.

Anzu Hanashiro

Female Protagonist

A strong-willed and determined transfer student. She decides to help Kaoru uncover the mystery of the tunnel while facing her own dilemmas.

Karen Touno

Kaoru’s Younger Sister

Kaoru's beloved little sister, who passed away in an accident years before the events of the story. Her death deeply affects Kaoru, and the possibility of seeing her again becomes a key motivation for him when he discovers the tunnel.

The Angel Next Door Spoils Me Rotten

2023

Mahiru Shiina

Main Protagonist

Known as "The Angel" of the school, she is a perfect girl in every aspect: intelligent, beautiful, and skilled at housework. Despite her distant appearance, she grows closer to Amane and begins to show a more affectionate side..

Amane Fujimiya

Male Protagonist

An ordinary and somewhat careless boy who lives alone. After a chance encounter with Mahiru, she starts helping him with his daily life, and the two develop a special bond.

Itsuki Akazawa

Amane’s best friend

Outgoing and playful, he supports Amane in his relationship with Mahiru.

Chitose Shirakawa

Itsuki’s girlfriend

Cheerful and energetic, she is a good friend of Mahiru and loves teasing Amane about his feelings.
